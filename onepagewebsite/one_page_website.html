WHAT SHOULD I WATCH TONIGHT?

We've all been there. You're home from a long day at work, the kids are finally asleep, you have your favorite blanket wrapped around you, a bowl of fresh popcorn on the table, and a glass of wine in your hand. Your significant other plops down on the couch next to you, and you're both excited to get into a new show together in order to tune out the stresses of the day. And then it happens. The dread. The anxiety. WHAT SHOULD I WATCH???

You flicker through the shows anxiously, skimming through the summaries, but alas! Nothing seems to satisfy both of you! The horror! The sadness! After 25 minutes of frustrated scrolling you both give up and scroll mindlessly through your phones until you black out.

Cue in What Should I Watch Tonight (also known as WSIWT, pronounced "wiz-ee-wit")! We understand your frustration, and have compiled a complete list of our favorite series, organized by genre. You know, because sometimes you want a quick 20 minute laugh, and sometimes you want to sink into total despair in a different time period. Check through our listings BEFORE you pick up that clicker, and never suffer through "what should I watch" syndrome again!
